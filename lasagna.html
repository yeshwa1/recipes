Lasagna

Description

This delicious lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta,
rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, and melted cheese.
It's a hearty and comforting meal that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef with diced onion and minced garlic. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. In a baking dish, layer the ingredients: start with a layer of meat sauce, followed by lasagna noodles,
    ricotta cheese mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used,
    ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  6. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional
    10 minutes
    or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  7. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
